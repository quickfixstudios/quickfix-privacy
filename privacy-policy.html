3. Information We Collect

When you interact with our Messenger bot, the following information may be processed:

  • Your Facebook name and Messenger user ID (PSID) — provided automatically by Facebook
  • The content of messages you send to the bot
  • The date and time of your messages
We do not collect sensitive personal health data, payment information, or store your conversation history permanently.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected solely to:

  • Respond to your questions about our apps and health topics
  • Recommend the most relevant QuickFix Studios app for your needs
  • Improve the quality and accuracy of bot responses
  • Connect you with our support team when necessary

5. Third-Party Services

Our bot uses the following third-party services to function:

  • Facebook / Meta Messenger Platform — for message delivery
  • OpenAI API — to generate intelligent responses to your queries
  • Make.com (Integromat) — for automation and workflow processing

Messages are processed transiently through these platforms. We encourage you to review their respective privacy policies for more details.

6. Data Retention

We do not permanently store your conversation data. Messages are processed in real-time to generate responses and are not retained in any database controlled by us after processing is complete.

7. Data Deletion

You have the right to request deletion of any data associated with your interactions with our bot. To submit a data deletion request:

  • Email us at msisabirsabirmsi@gmail.com with the subject line: "Data Deletion Request"

You can also stop all data processing at any time by simply blocking or unfollowing our Facebook Page.

8. Health Disclaimer

The Bot provides general health information only and does not constitute medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for medical decisions. In case of emergencies, contact your nearest hospital immediately.

9. Children's Privacy

Our bot is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has interacted with our bot, please contact us immediately.
